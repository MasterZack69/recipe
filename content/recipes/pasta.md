## Ingredients

- 250g spaghetti
- 2 tbsp olive oil
- 2 garlic cloves, minced
- Zest of 1 lemon
- 1 tbsp lemon juice
- 2 tbsp chopped parsley
- Salt and black pepper

## Instructions

1. Cook spaghetti in salted water until al dente.
2. Warm olive oil in a pan and sauté garlic for 30 seconds.
3. Add drained pasta, lemon zest, and lemon juice. Toss to coat.
4. Stir in parsley, season with salt and pepper, and serve immediately.
